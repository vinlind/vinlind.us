I use a custom resource pack based on a 5-year old scuffed version of “Faithful”

Info

I don’t exactly remember where this pack came from, but it’s an old version of Faithful from around 2015. Because it’s that old, it uses the revived Faithful pack for textures past 1.8. It’s also got PvP-optimized textures & birch trees are converted to cherry trees.
 
Without Optifine, connected textures don’t work. Installing ConnectedTexturesMod and putting “FaithfulCustom CTM Glass” above FaithfulCustom will re-enable connected glass.
